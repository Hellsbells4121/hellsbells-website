Atherton Tablelands, Far North Queensland

Hi, I'm HP.

I crochet, I write, I make music, and I stream most of it live. I've been crocheting for 56 years, and somewhere along the way that turned into Hellsbells — a small brand for all the things I make, tied together by a rainforest palette of copper and teal.

The crochet

Decades of practice, now streamed live — fantasy amigurumi built from a 14-pattern book, with the audience voting on colours and creatures.

The music

Ambient electronica made in the quiet hours, released as HPoulos71 / Hellsbells4121.

The stories

Writing as Briony Bramble, I set gentle bedtime adventures in the real rainforest around Lake Eacham and Mount Hypipamee.

The stream

All of it comes together live on Twitch — crafting, chat, and the odd raffle for a hand-stitched creature.

Come say hi

I'm usually online somewhere between a crochet hook and a synth patch.
